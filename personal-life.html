Personal Life

He married Mary Todd, and they had four boys only one of whom lived to maturity.

In 1858 Lincoln ran against Stephen A. Doulas for senator.

He lost this election, but because of what he said during debates, he ended up becoming a favorite. He used that reputation to run as president and he ended up winning.

He was the first ever president to be assassinated, but not the first president to die in office.
